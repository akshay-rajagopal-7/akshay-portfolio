AureviaForge Engine

Forensic audit analytics platform
● Live

A full forensic audit pipeline in the browser. Auditors upload raw transactional data and the engine automatically surfaces exceptions and anomalies, ranks them, and lets investigators attach persistent case notes to every finding — building a complete, exportable evidence trail. An integrated AI reasoning layer explains why each item was flagged in plain language, turning a wall of numbers into a defensible narrative. Built to handle large result sets with paginated findings (200 at a time) and one-click CSV export of findings and notes for working papers.

Darvin & the Pulse

Three-agent AI review tribunal
Beta

An adversarial AI tribunal that puts every forensic finding on trial before it ever reaches a report. Three distinct AI agents argue the case: Darvin prosecutes as a fraud examiner, Ward defends, and Athena weighs both sides and rules as chief arbiter. The result is a finding that has survived genuine scrutiny rather than a single model's first guess — a built-in second opinion that reduces false positives and strengthens the audit conclusion. Convened directly from any flagged exception in AureviaForge, with a courtroom-style interface, animated agents, and per-agent voice narration.

Petty Cash Workbook

Advanced Excel model · AED
Spreadsheet

A production-grade petty-cash control workbook built for real project finance in AED. It tracks every transaction, reconciles staff advances against actual spend, flags variances automatically, and rolls up to live closing balances — all formula-driven, so the books stay accurate without manual recalculation. A dedicated guide sheet makes it hand-off ready for any team member. This is the spreadsheet discipline that informs the forensic engine: the same controls, made auditable.
