AureviaForge Engine

forensic_audit_analytics
by Akshay Rajagopal

A forensic analytics engine that processes up to 100 million financial transactions in under a minute — an entire enterprise's multi-year history, scanned end to end and surfaced for the red flags that actually matter. It runs ten investigative tests in parallel, scores and ranks every exception by risk so the most serious issues rise to the top, and lets you attach case notes against each finding and export the full audit trail to CSV for review or reporting. On top of that sits an AI layer you can interrogate in plain language — ask why a transaction was flagged, request a summary of a vendor's activity, or generate an executive-ready brief in seconds, all grounded in the underlying data rather than guesswork. The result is a faster, sharper first pass than any manual review, with every conclusion traceable back to the evidence.

Darvin & the Pulse

adversarial_ai_tribunal
by Akshay Rajagopal

Most tools just read your numbers and hand you a list. This one puts every finding on trial. Think of it as a courtroom built from three AI minds: one acts as the prosecutor and argues the transaction is suspicious, a second acts as the defence and argues it's perfectly innocent, and a third sits as the judge and decides who's right. Nothing reaches your final report until it has survived that fight. The result? You stop chasing false alarms, the real problems stand out clearly, and every conclusion comes with the reasoning behind it — so when someone asks "how do you know?", you have an answer you can actually defend in front of a board, an auditor, or a regulator.
